This archive contains the platform library of the RobotRace assignment of the Computer Graphics course.

Version:	2
Last update:	19-11-2012

Changes in version 2:
- The X and Y components of GlobalState.cnt are no longer reset to zero when the `Q' or `Z' key is pressed;
- The bounds of the camera parameters GlobalState.theta, GlobalState.cnt, and GlobalState.vWidth have been widened.
